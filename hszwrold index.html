How it works

1) Connect GA4: You grant read-only access to your GA4 property.

2) Add context: Describe your business model, goals, and key conversions.

3) Ask questions: Natural language → relevant metrics → interpretation → next steps.

4) Act: You get recommendations that map to channels, pages, devices, and funnels.

Examples of questions

• Where is my best traffic coming from, and why?

• Which channel brings engaged users, not just clicks?

FAQ

Do you store my GA4 data?

By default, the app should only access what’s needed to answer your question. Implementation details may evolve; see the Privacy Policy for the current approach.

What access do you need?

Read-only access to your GA4 property (so the assistant can query metrics and dimensions).

Is this an official Google product?

No. G.Ai Analyst is an independent tool built to work with Google Analytics 4.
